The Waybook

Define your own unique and meaningful path

  • Discover

    Who, What, Why?

    Whether it's truly understanding your basic personality, your principals, or where you would like to work, Way provides you with the tools and resources you need to figure it out.

  • Plan

    Commit to making it happen.

    There's something magical about setting goals. There's more magical about finding the resources you need to accomplish your goals. Way can help you with both. It's your life. You are in control.

  • Unite

    Call on the people who care.

    Whether it's a mentor, coach, parent, teacher, or friend. There are people who care about your success. If you don't know who they are, Way can help you find them.

If you want to be true to yourself, you truly have to understand yourself. - Way

Platform

Leverage the Waybook to serve your learners, your way

Platform

Leverage the Waybook to serve your learners, your way

  • Mentors

    Your own App (almost)

    The Waybook ultimately belongs to the learner. It's their lifelong companion. However, besides having a positive effect on the direction of their life, you can also embed your best practices in their Waybook. While they are with you, the Waybook can be a positive reflection of your brand. The Waybook can be your gift to your clients.

  • Schools

    Help your students find their path

    Waybook enables schools, counselors, and teachers, to facilitate, and embed themselves in the planning and success of your students future. The Waybook is everything you need to put what you offer in context for the student. Furthermore, it's your app (almost!; it's ultimately the students lifelong companion. As a school, you maintain a significant presence for part of the journey.

  • Education Resource Developers

    Embedd our APIs and Visualizations

    Do you have multiple learning resources that together form one or more curriculum? When not put these in the context of the learner, instead of asking the learner to put themselves in the context of your business? Leverage Way's RESTful APIs and embed scrips to build learning maps, and create beautiful visualizations of same. Way intends to support standards and application scenarios emerging from inBloom.org.

A positive reflection on your brand that is the learner's lifelong companion, in their context first and foremost, accessible to all.
