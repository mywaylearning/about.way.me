Learner + Guide = rolling 10 year plan

Way can be used without a Guide, or you can work with your friends, family, and teachers as guides. However, sometimes you want a focused, motivated, and credentialed professional to guide you to the right questions. The basic function of a Guide is to facilitate better self awareness, guide you through the creation of your own unique and relevant short term and long term plans, and guide you in determining which resources will best help you succeed in achieving your goals (e.g. people, online resources, and experiences).

Platform

  • Guides, Coaches, Mentors

    Your own App (almost)

    The WaybookTM is like having your own app. Embed your best practices in your client's WaybookTM and achieve scale. Discover and leverage new best practices. While the WaybookTM ultimately belongs to the learner, it can be a positive reflection of your brand; your gift to your clients.

  • Schools

    Help your students find their path

    WaybookTM enables schools, counselors, and teachers, to facilitate, and embed themselves in the planning and success of your student's future. The WaybookTM is a cost effective way to light up an IEP for each and every student and provide them with continuity across grades and schools. Put what you offer in the context of the student, for the student.

  • Educational Resource Developers

    Embedd our APIs and Visualizations

    Do you have multiple learning resources that together form one or more curriculum? Why not put these in the context of the learner, instead of asking the learner to put themselves in the context of your business? Leverage Way's RESTful APIs and embed scripts to build learning maps, and create beautiful visualizations of same. Way intends to support standards and application scenarios emerging from inBloom.org.
